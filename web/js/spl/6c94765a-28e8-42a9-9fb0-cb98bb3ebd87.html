MEDICATED SHAMPOO

Medicated Shampoo may be used as extra therapy for common dermatological conditions. Removes scales and crust as well as being antibacterial and antifungal. Ideal for deodorizing and gentle wash for routine use.﻿

Directions for Use:

Shake well. Thoroughly wet animal's coat with tepid water. Massage small amounts of shampoo into the coat and continuously add water to better disperse product, until a generous lather is generated. Rinse well and repeat. Shampoo may be used weekly or as recommended by your veterinarian.

Active Ingredients:

2% Chloroxylenol, 2% Salicylic Acid, 2% Sodium Thiosulfate (source of soluble sulfur).

Ingredients:

Deionized water, Sodium C14-16 Olefin Sulfonate, Propylene Glycol, Disodium Cocoamphodiacetate, Polyquaternium-10, Fragrance, Citric Acid, Methylchloroisothiazolinone, Methylisothiazolinone, FD&C Blue No. 1.

Precautions:

For external use only. Keep out of eyes. If contact occurs, flush with water immediately. Keep out of reach of children. Keep at room temperature.
