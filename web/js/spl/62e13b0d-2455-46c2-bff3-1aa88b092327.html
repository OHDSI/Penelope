DHS ZINC

WARNINGS AND PRECAUTIONS

For external use only. Ask a physician before use if you have Psoriasis or Seborrheic Dermatitis that covers a large area of the body. Do not get into eyes. If contact occurs, rinse eyes thoroughly with water. Stop use and ask a physician if condition worsens or does not improve after regular use.

Dosage and Administration

Shake well before using.  Wet hair thoroughly. Apply a liberal amount of shampoo and massage into a rich lather. Allow lather to remain on scalp for several minutes. Rinse hair well and repeat application. For best results use at least twice a week or as directed by a physician.

Indications and Use

Helps prevent recurrence of scalp itching and flaking associated with Seborrheic Dermatitis and Dandruff.

OTC - KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN

Yes.  If swallowed, get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away.

PURPOSE

Recommended for control of Dandruff of Seborrheic Dermatitis
