Drug Facts

Active ingredients:
Acetaminophen   500mg
Pseudoephedrine HCl   30mg   
Chlorpheniramine Maleate   3mg

Inactive ingredients:
lactose, corn starch, microcrystalline cellulose, Hydroxypropylcellulose, magnesium stearate, hydroxypropyl methylcellulose, titanium oxide, propylene glycol

Uses:
The relief of common cold symptoms(nasal mucus, nasal obstruction, sneeze, throat pain, chill, fever, headache, arthralgia, myalgia)

Direction:
Adults and children 12 years and over: take 2 tables every 6 hours, not to exceed 8 tables in 24 hours

Indication for usage:
Adults and children 12 years and over: take 2 tables every 6 hours, not to exceed 8 tables in 24 hours

Warnings:
Ask a doctor or pharmacist before use if you are regularly drinking over three cups of alcohol per day or taking any other antipyretic and analgesics. Liver can be damaged if taken.

Caution if taken
  1) Keep direction
  2) Not taken with anti-tussive, expectorant, antipyretic, antihistamine, sedative
  3) Let children take drug by protector
  4) Not taken below 3 months. If not mandatory, do not let child take drug between 3 months and one year.
  5) Stop taking medicine and ask a doctor or pharmacist before use even though adverse events are rarely reported below.
    (1) Rash, edema, breast burden, pale complexion, cold hand and foot, cold sweat, shortness of breath
    (2) High fever, rash, flare, bubble on whole skin, mouth, eye mucosa
    (3) Asthma
  6) Barbital induced drug, tri-cyclic antidepressant and alcohol can reduce ability of acetaminophen metabolism and increase plasma half life. Alcohol could increase liver toxicity of acetaminophen overdose

Caution if taken
  1) While taking a rash, redness, nausea, vomiting, constipation, anorexia, dysuria, dizziness and other symptoms when you stop asking your doctor or pharmacist
  2) If you get drowsy, Be careful when driving a motor vehicle or operating machinery
  3) Please don’t take drug and ask doctor or pharmacist if you can not feel better by several dose.
  4) Don’t take for a long term

Contraindication:
  1) If you are anaphylactic to this drug (e.g., rash, flare, pruritis, edema)
  2) Asthma patient after taking this drug or other anti-cold, antipyretic and analgesic.

Ask a doctor or pharmacist before use
  1) Family history to urticaria, contact dermatitis, bronchial asthma, allergic rhinitis, migrane, food allergy
  2) Allergy experience to this drug (fever, rash, arthritis, asthma, pruritis)
  3) Child below 14 years may be Infected by Chickenpox or influenza
  4) Disease of Liver, kidney and thyroid disease, Diabetes, hypertension, weakling, high-fever
  5) Glaucoma, dysuria
  6) Pregnant or breast-feeding
  7) People treated by MD or dentist

Caution of storage:
  1) Keep out of reach of children
  2) Avoid sunlight and keep in cool and low humid place
  3) Don’t abuse and replace with other container

Keep out of reach of children:
Keep out of reach of children

Other information:
Store in a hermetic container. Store between 1℃ to 30℃. 36 months from the manufacturing date
