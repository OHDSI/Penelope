DEXTROSE MONOHYDRATE

• CONTENTS (grams/100ml):

  • Dextrose as monohydrate .....50

Inactives:

  • Purified Water  q.s.
  • Methylparaben
  • Propylparaben

Indications:

  • For use as a supplemental nutritive source of dextrose in cattle.

Storage:

  • Store at controlled room temperature between 59oF to 86oF (15oC -30oC).

Warnings:

  • Avoid storage at cold temperatures which can cause product to become cloudy.
  • To clarify place product in warm water bath (100oF-120oF) for about 30 minutes.

Dosage and administration:

  • Administer orally as a drench or by use of a stomach tube.
  • The usual recommended dose in adult cattle is 16.9 fl. oz (500mL) to 33.8 fl.oz (1000mL) depending on size and condition.

  • Keep away from children.
