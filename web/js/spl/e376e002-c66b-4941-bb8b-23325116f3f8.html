American Sales Company Pain Relief Cream Drug Facts

Active ingredient

Camphor 4%

Menthol 10%

Methyl salicylate 30%

Purpose

Topical analgesic

Uses

temporarily relieves the minor aches and pains of muscles and joints associated with

  • simple backache
  • arthritis
  • strains
  • bruises
  • sprains

Warnings

For external use only

Do not use

other than as directed

When using this product

  • avoid contact with the eyes or mucous membranes
  • do not apply to wounds or damaged skin
  • do not bandage tightly
  • do not use with a heating pad

Stop use and ask a doctor if

  • condition worsens or symptoms persist for more than 7 days
  • symptoms clear up and occur again within a few days
  • redness is present or excessive skin irritation develops

Keep out of reach of children.

If swallowed, get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away. (1-800-222-1222)

Directions

  • use only as directed
  • adults & children 12 years of age and older: apply to affected area not more than 3 to 4 times daily
  • children under 12 years of age: ask a doctor

Other information

store at 20°-25°C (68°-77°F)

Inactive ingredients

carbomer homopolymer type C, edetate disodium, glyceryl monostearate, lanolin anhydrous, PEG-100 stearate, polysorbate 80, potassium hydroxide, purified water, stearic acid, trolamine
