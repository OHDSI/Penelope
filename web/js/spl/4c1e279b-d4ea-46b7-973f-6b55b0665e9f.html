Aveeno Baby Organic Harvest Diaper Rash Cream

Drug Facts

Active ingredient(s)

Zinc Oxide 13%

Purposes

Skin Protectant

Uses

  • Helps treat and prevent diaper rash
  • Protects chafed skin due to diaper rash
  • Helps seal out wetness

Warnings

For external use only.

When using this product

  • Do not get into eyes

Stop use and ask a doctor if

  • Condition worsens
  • Symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur within a few days.

Keep out of reach of children. If swallowed, get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away.

Directions

  • Change wet or soiled diapers promptly
  • Clean the diaper area
  • allow to dry
  • Apply cream liberally as often as necessary, with each diaper change, especially at bedtime or any time prolonged exposure to wet diapers may be prolonged.

Other Information

Store at 20°C to 25°C (68°F to 77°F) Remove quality seal before use. Do not use if quality sal is broken.

Inactive ingredients

Helianthus Annuus (Sunflower) Seed Oil, Polyglyceryl-3 Beeswax, Beeswax, Silica, Tapioca Starch, Avena Sativa (Oat) Kernel Extract
