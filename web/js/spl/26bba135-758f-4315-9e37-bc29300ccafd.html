WHEN USING THIS PRODUCT

avoid contact with eyes
ask you doctor to check your INR more frequently if you are taking anticoagulants

DO NOT USE

do not use if you are allergic to salicylates, aspirin, or any of the inactive ingredients

do not use if seal is broken or is not visible

Stop use and ask a doctor if

condition worsens
redness is present
irritation develops
symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days

keep out of reach of children

If swallowed, get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away.

If pregnant or breast-feeding

ask a health professional before use.

storage

store under 30C (86F)

inactive ingredients

isopropyl alcohol, glycerol, peppermint oil, purified water

directions for use

Apply bluMjk pain relief liberally to a wide area covering a circle with a diameter of at least four (4) inches from the point of maximum pain. Keep painful area at reat for ten (10) minutes after application. If pain persists after the first application, reapply. You may reapply every four (4) to six (6) hours as needed. Begin with a liberal amount, reduce with each use, and maintain the effective amount for optimum results.
