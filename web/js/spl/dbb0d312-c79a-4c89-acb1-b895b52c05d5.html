Avitears Lubricating Eye Drops

Drug Facts

Active Ingredient

Polysorbate 80, 1%

Purpose

Lubricant

Uses

  • Temporary relief of discomfort, burning and irritation due to dryness of the eye or from exposure to wind, sun, computer use, environmental conditions, prescription medication, post-surgical dry eye or systemically induced dry eye.
  • Lubricant and protectant from further irritation

Warnings

  • For external use only
  • To avoid contamination replace cap after use
  • Do not touch the tip of the container to any surface to avoid contamination
  • Do not use if solution changes color or becomes cloudy

Stop use and ask a doctor

  • If you experience eye pain, changes in vision, continued redness or irritation, or if condition persists for more than 72 hours or worsens
  • Keep this and all other drugs out of the reach of children

Directions

  • Instill one or two drops in the affected eye(s) as needed

Other Information

Store at 20 to 25° C (68 to 77° F)

Controlled USP Room Temperature

Inactive Ingredients

Citric acid, edetate disodium, WFI water, sodium chloride and the antioxidants: mannitol, pyruvate, retinyl palmitate and sodium citrate.
