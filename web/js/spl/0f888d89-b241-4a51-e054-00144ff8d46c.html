CAMPHO-PHENIQUE COLD SORE TREATMENT

Drug Facts

Camphorated phenol (camphor 10.8% and phenol 4.7%)

Pain reliever/antiseptic

  • for temporary relief of pain and itching associated with cold sores and fever blisters
  • first aid to help prevent infection

For external use only

  • over large areas of the body
  • with a bandage

  • a deep or puncture wound
  • animal bites
  • serious burns

do not use in or near the eyes. If contact occurs, rinse eyes thoroughly with water and obtain medical attention.

Stop use and ask a doctor if

  • condition worsens
  • symptoms persist for more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days

Keep out of reach of children. If swallowed, get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away.

Do not induce vomiting before contacting medical help or a Poison Control Center.

  • adults and children 2 years and over:
  • clean the affected area
  • apply a small amount of this product to the cold sore or fever blister 1 to 3 times daily
  • do not bandage
  • children under 2 years: ask a doctor

  • store at room temperature. Avoid excessive heat above 40°C (104°F).
  • close cap tightly after use
