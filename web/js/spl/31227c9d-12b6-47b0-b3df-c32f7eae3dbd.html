PET PECTILLIN Diarrhea Medication (​pectin​ and ​kaolin​) helps quickly relieve diarrhea or loose stool in dogs and cats. Aids in the recovery from dehydration caused by diarrhea by replenishing electrolytes. Provides a protective coating to calm the digestive tract and absorb toxins. Helps alleviate irritation, discomfort and cramping associated with diarrhea. Increases the bulk and slows down the passage of feces.

Warnings:

​Do not use on pregnant dogs or cats

Keep out of reach of animals and children

Storage:

Store at temperatures between 65 to 85F.

Do not freeze.

DIRECTIONS FOR USE

SHAKE WELL BEFORE USING

DOGS AND CATS:

Mix with food or feed directly every 4 hours.

Up to 15 lb (6.8 kg)...........................1 teaspoon (5 mL)

15 lbg (6.8 kg) to 50 lb (22.7 kg)......2 teaspoons (10 mL)

Over 50 lb (22.7 kg)..........................1 tablespoon (15 mL)

CAUTION:

If symptoms persist after use for 2 to 3 days, consult your veterinarian.

​NOT FOR HUMAN CONSUMPTION

KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN

USE ONLY AS DIRECTED
