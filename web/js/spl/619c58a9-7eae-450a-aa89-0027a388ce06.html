CAUTION! HIGH-PRESSURE GAS. MAY ACCELERATE COMBUSTION. CONCENTRATIONS BELOW 19.5% OXYGEN CAN CAUSE RAPID ASPHYXIATION MAY CAUSE DIZZINESS AND DROWSINESS.

Keep oil, grease, and combustibles away. Keep away from heat, sparks, and flame. Store and use with adequate ventilation. Use with equipment rated for cylinder pressure and cleaned for oxygen service. Use a backflow prevention device in any piping. Cylinder temperature should not exceed 125°F (52°C). Open valve slowly. Close valve after each use; keep closed even when empty. Always secure cylinder. Install cap, if provided, when not in use. Use per Praxair MSDS provided and safe practices booklets P-3499 and P-14-153; obtain from your local supplier.

FIRST AID: IF INHALED, remove to fresh air. If not breathing, give artificial respiration. If breathing is difficult, qualified personnel may give oxygen. Call a physician.
