Earoxide™ PET EAR CLEANSER

Earoxide™ by Tomlyn® ear cleanser is a safe and easy way to dissolve and wash away dirt and wax from your dog or cat's ears – instantly and painlessly.

DIRECTIONS

Clip ear hair and clean your pet's outer ear. Fill the ear canal with Earoxide™ and gently massage by pressing on the external ear canal. After a few minutes, you may release your pet and allow it to shake its head and throw out the dissolved material. Repeat daily until the ear canal is free of foreign material.

NO SWABBING OR PROBING OF THE EAR CANAL IS REQUIRED.

CAUTION

If draining or irritation persists, consult your veterinarian.

FOR ANIMAL USE ONLY.

KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN AND PETS.

INGREDIENTS

Contains carbamide peroxide 6.5% in a stabilized glycerin base.

STORE IN A COOL DRY PLACE. KEEP CAP TIGHTLY CLOSED.
