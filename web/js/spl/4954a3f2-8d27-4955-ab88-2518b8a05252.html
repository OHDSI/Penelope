Step 1: Treat

Lice Killing Shampoo

NOTE:

‚Ä¢ Use towels to protect child's eyes from treatment and prevent clothes from getting wet.

‚Ä¢ Apply Lice Killing Shampoo thoroughly to DRYHAIR or other affected area.

‚Ä¢ Also, lice can hold their breath when immersed in water making it difficult for the active ingredient to penetrate the lice.
