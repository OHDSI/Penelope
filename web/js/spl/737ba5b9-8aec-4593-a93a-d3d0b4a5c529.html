DESCRIPTION

TL Gard Rx are round, light pink, speckled, film-coated tablets debossed "TL015" and intended for oral administration.

Supplement Facts
Servings per Bottle: 100
Serving Size: 1 tablet
Each tablet contains		 % DV for Adults Only
Folic Acid 2.2 mg 550%
Vitamin B6 (pyridoxine HCl) 25 mg 1250%
Vitamin B12 (cyanocobalamin) 1 mg 167%

Inactive Ingredients:
Microcrystalline Cellulose, Di Cal Phosphate, TriPotassium Citrate, Citric Acid, Sodium Carboxymethyl Cellulose, Stearic Acid, Silicon Dioxide, Dextrin, Dextrose (corn), Lecithin, Sodium Citrate.

INDICATIONS AND USAGE

TL Gard Rx tablets are indicated for nutritional support and folic acid supplementation.

CONTRAINDICATIONS

This product is contraindicated in patients with known history of hypersensitivity to any of the ingredients.

THIS PRODUCT CONTAINS SOY.

PRECAUTIONS

Folic acid when taken in doses above 0.1 mg daily, may obscure pernicious anemia in that hematologic remission can occur while neurological manifestations remain progressive.

Pediatric Use

Safety and effectiveness in pediatric patients have not been established.

ADVERSE REACTIONS

Allergic sensitization has been reported following both oral and parenteral administration of folic acid.

DOSAGE AND ADMINISTRATION

Adults - one tablet daily or as prescribed by a physician.

HOW SUPPLIED

TL Gard Rx tablets are available in bottles of 100 tablets.

STORAGE

Store at 20°-25°C (68°-77°F), excursions permitted to 15°-30°C (59°-86°F) [See USP controlled room temperature]. Protect from light and moisture.

Dispense in a tight container with a child-resistant closure.

Call your doctor about side effects. You may report side effects by calling 888 9 TRIGEN (888-987-4436).
