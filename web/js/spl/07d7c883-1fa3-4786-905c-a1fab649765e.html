Monistat Complete Care™ Chafing Relief Powder Gel®

Drug Facts

Active Ingredient

Dimethicone 1.2%

Purpose

Skin Protectant

Use

  • temporarily protects and helps relieve chafed, chapped or cracked skin.

Warnings

For external use only

When using this product

  • do not get into eyes

Stop use and ask a doctor if

  • condition worsens
  • symptoms last more than 7 days or clear up and occur again within a few days

Keep out of reach of children. If swallowed get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away.

Directions

Apply as needed.

Inactive Ingredients

Cyclopentasiloxane, dimethicone/vinyl dimethicone crosspolymer, silica, tocopheryl acetate, trisiloxane
