ELECTROLYTE POWDER 8X

DIRECTIONS FOR USE

Dissolve one package (16 oz.) in 40 gallons of drinking water.

WARNING

This product contains citrate salts which in some cases may be incompatible with tetracycline antibiotics.

TAKE TIME OBSERVE LABEL DIRECTIONS

STORAGE


Do not store above 30oC (86oF)
