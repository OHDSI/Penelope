ACTIVE INGREDIENT

Pramoxine Hydrochloride 1%

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION:

 Relief® Creme Rinse combines Pramoxine and micronized colloidal oatmeal in a soothing formulation containing moisturizers and emollients. Contains Omega-6 fatty acids for replenishment of the epidermal barrier.

INDICATIONS:

For use as an after bath creme rinse for temporary relief of itching, flaking and irritation.

DIRECTIONS:

Shake well before use. After routine or medicated shampooing, apply Relief ® Creme Rinse evenly and thoroughly into hair coat. Rub in well. May be used as a leave-on conditioner or lightly rinse the hair coat after 5 to 10 minutes of contact time. May be used as often as necessary or as directed by your veterinarian.

CAUTIONS:

For topical use on animals only. If redness or irritation persists or increases, discontinue use and contact your veterinarian. Avoid contact with eyes. If eye contact occurs, rinse thoroughly with water. Available through licensed veterinarians only.

STORAGE:

Store at room temperature. Protect from freezing
