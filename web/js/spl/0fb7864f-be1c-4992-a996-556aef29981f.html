10Â Â Â Â Â  OVERDOSAGE

In adults following a single administration of Zingoâ„¢ the plasma levels of lidocaine were below the limit of detection (5 ng/mL). The management of overdosage includes close monitoring, supportive care, and symptomatic treatment. Dialysis is of negligible value in the treatment of acute overdosage of lidocaine.
