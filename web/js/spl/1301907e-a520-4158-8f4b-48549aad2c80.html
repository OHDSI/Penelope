ACTIVE INGREDIENT:

Pramoxine Hydrochloride 1%

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION:

Relief® Shampoo is a soap-free, cleansing formulation combining the soothing benefits of Pramoxine and micronized colloidal oatmeal. Contains Omega-6 fatty acids for replenishment of the

epidermal barrier.

INDICATIONS:

For cleansing of the hair coat and temporary relief of itching, flaking and irritation

DIRECTIONS:

Shake well before use. Wet coat thoroughly. Apply sufficient Relief® Shampoo to lather well into hair coat. Contact with the skin is important. When entire coat is treated, allow lather to remain for 5 to 10 minutes. Rinse thoroughly with water. May be used as often as necessary or as directed by your veterinarian

CAUTIONS:

For topical use on animals only. If redness or irritation persists or increases, discontinue use and contact your veterinarian. Avoid contact with eyes. If eye contact occurs, rinse thoroughly with water. Available through licensed veterinarians only.

STORAGE:

Store at room temperature.

Protect from freezing.
