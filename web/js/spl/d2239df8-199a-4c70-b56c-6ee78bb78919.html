Health Claim

For use as a dietary supplement in adults.

Active Ingredient:
Each tablet contains 500 mg calcium gluconate (equivalent to 45 mg elemental calcium).

Inactive Ingredients:
Magnesium stearate and microcrystalline cellulose.

Warnings

As with any supplement, if you are pregnant or nursing a baby, contact your healthcare professional.

Do not use if printed foil under cap is broken or missing.

Precautions

Keep bottle tightly closed. Store in a cool, dry place, out of reach of children. In case of overdose, get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately.

Safe Handling

Store at 20° to 25°C (68° to 77°F). [See USP Controlled Room Temperature.]

Dosage and Administration

Take 1 to 16 tablets per day, in divided doses, preferably 1 to 2 hours after meals, or as directed by a physician.

Questions or Comments?

Call 1-800-962-8364. You may also report serious side effects to this phone number.
