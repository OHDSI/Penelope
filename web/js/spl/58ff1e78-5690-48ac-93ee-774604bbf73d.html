SuperValu Inc. Triple Antibiotic Ointment Drug Facts

Active ingredients (in each gram)

Bacitracin 400 units

Neomycin sulfate equivalent to 3.5 mg of neomycin base

Polymyxin B 5,000 units as polymyxin B sulfate

Purpose

First aid antibiotic

Use

first aid to help prevent infection in minor:

•
cuts
•
scrapes
•
burns

Warnings

For external use only.

Do not use

•
if you are allergic to any of the ingredients
•
in the eyes
•
over large areas of the body

Ask a doctor before use if you have

•
deep or puncture wounds
•
animal bites
•
serious burns

Stop use and ask a doctor if

•
you need to use longer than 1 week
•
condition persists or gets worse
•
rash or other allergic reaction develops

Keep out of reach of children.

If swallowed, get medical help or contact a Poison Control Center right away. (1-800-222-1222)

Directions

•
clean the affected area
•
apply a small amount of this product (an amount equal to the surface area of the tip of a finger) on the area 1 to 3 times daily
•
may be covered with a sterile bandage

Other information

•
store at 20°-25°C (68°-77°F)

Inactive ingredients

light mineral oil, white petrolatum
