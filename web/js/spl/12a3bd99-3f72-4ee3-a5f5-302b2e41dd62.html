Accel Gold Kote

 

AN EXCELLENT AID FOR CONTROLLING MASTITIS

 

DIRECTIONS: Immediately after milking and before the cow is

released, dip each teat in undiluted ACCEL GOLD-KOTE

barrier teat dip. Teats should be dipped or sprayed to the base

of the udder. If the dip becomes visibly dirty or contains

sediment, replace with fresh solution. At the end of lactation, the

teats should be dipped or sprayed with ACCEL GOLD-KOTE

for four days after the last milking. Teats should be thoroughly

washed and dried before milking. Use potable water, a suitable

cleanser and individual paper towels to remove the barrier and

to prevent contamination of the milk. Do not use for cleaning or

sanitizing milk equipment.

 

WARNING: Can cause eye damage. Harmful if swallowed.

Do not get in eyes or on clothing. Avoid contamination of food.

 

FIRST AID: In case of contact with eyes, flush immediately with

plenty of water for at least 15 minutes. Call a physician.

 

INTERNAL: If swallowed, induce vomiting and call a

physician immediately.

 

CAUTION: Avoid freezing. If frozen, let thaw and shake

thoroughly before using.
