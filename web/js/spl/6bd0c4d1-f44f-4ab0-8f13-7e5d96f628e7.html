RALGRO- zeranol implant 
Merck Sharp & Dohme Corp.

RALGRO®

Contains ten 24-dose cartridges. (Dosage: 3 pellets, each containing 12 mg of Zeranol)

NDC 0061-5026-03

For increased weight gain and for improved feed conversion of weaned beef calves, growing beef cattle, feedlot steers and feedlot heifers. For increased rate of weight gain of suckling beef calves.

Do not use in veal calves. Effectiveness and animal safety in veal calves have not been established.

Implants for Beef Cattle

WARNING

DO NOT ATTEMPT TO SALVAGE IMPLANT SITE FOR HUMAN OR ANIMAL FOOD. IMPLANT PELLETS AS DIRECTED IN THE EAR ONLY. ANY OTHER LOCATION IS IN VIOLATION OF FEDERAL LAW.

A WITHDRAWAL PERIOD HAS NOT BEEN ESTABLISHED FOR THIS PRODUCT IN PRE-RUMINATING CALVES. DO NOT USE IN CALVES TO BE PROCESSED FOR VEAL.

CAUTION

DO NOT IMPLANT BULLS INTENDED FOR REPRODUCTION OR DAIRY ANIMALS WITH ZERANOL. DO NOT USE BEFORE 1 MONTH OF AGE OR AFTER WEANING IN HEIFERS INTENDED FOR REPRODUCTION.

HAZARDOUS—NOT FOR HUMAN USE.

This product may not be marketed or used in the EU.

Restricted Drug (California),
Use Only as Directed.

See package insert for use directions.

Store between 2° and 25°C (36° and 77°F).
