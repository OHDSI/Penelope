ATOMY HERBAL HAIR TONIC

Drug Facts

Active ingredient
Panthenol  0.2%
Menthol  0.3%
Salicylic Acid  0.25%

Uses
give nutrition to the scalp, promote metabolism and prevent hair loss

Directions
-shake well before using product
-apply an appropriate amount all over scalp every day
-scrub and massage scalp using hands for helping to absorb product

Warnings
-For external use only.
-Do not use your scalp is red, inflamed, infected, irritated or painful
-When using this product : Do not apply on other parts of the body. Avoid contact with the eyes. In case of accidental contact, rinse eyes with large amounts of cool tap water.
-Stop use and ask a doctor if scalp irritation or redness occurs
-Keep out of reach of children if swallowed

Other information
Store at room temperature. Avoid direct exposure to sunlight.
